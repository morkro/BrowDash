What happens to my data?

Your data is saved locally in your current browser through the localStorage API. No cookies are in use nor will your data be uploaded somewhere.

That means you have full power about your data. If you want to delete or just review everything, open the Chrome Developer Tools and go to the Resources tab. Under Local Storage you can see all your saved data.
